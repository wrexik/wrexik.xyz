Notes & Workflow

Obsidian - Notekeeping and work management. Lots of addons. 9/10
Notion - To-Do, management, organizing. We use it to manage Esteris 9/10

AI

HuggingFace - Use AI applications for free. Download models and programs. 10/10
StabilityMatrix - Automatic installer and manager for self-hosted AI image models and tools. 10/10
Suno AI - AI-powered voice assistant for developers. Provides natural language understanding and voice command capabilities. 9/10

Okada VC - Real-time AI voice changer using RVC models. Cuda / CPU 10/10
Applio - Program for training your own model. Also used for AI song covers. With features like overtraining detection 10/10
OpenAI - ChatGPT, SoRa, and DALL•E as we all know it. 9/10
A1111 - Stable Diffusion web UI, also included in StabilityMatrix 8/10
UVR3 - Ultimate Vocal Remover, I personally use it in combination with Applio and the results are Amazing 10/10
LM Studio - Local AI model manager. I use it for local LLMs. Best out there right now. 10/10

Network Tools

Tailscale - Safe and free method to connect to your homelab or vps! It can also work for remote desktop & sunshine moonlight. 9/10
Curl - Command-line tool for making HTTP requests. Often used by me to see content of site from cli. 8/10
ngrok - Useful for testing webhooks and local development. I used it in now outdated project: Tunnel bot 9/10
Wireshark - View packets and analyze network traffic. 10/10
Angry IP Scanner - Great program for searching local devices. E.g., RPI. 7/10
Nmap - Network exploration and security analyzing tool. Often used to look for vulnerabilities. 10/10
Pi-hole - Network-wide ad blocker. I use it for my home lab. 10/10
OpenVPN - Open-source VPN software. 10/10
WireGuard - Fast and modern VPN protocol. I use it for my home lab. 10/10
Netcat - Networking utility for reading and writing data across network connections. 9/10

Office

LibreOffice - Free and open-source office apps. Preinstalled on Ubuntu 7/10
Office 365 - Cloud-based subscription service provided by Microsoft. Includes popular applications like Word, Excel, and PowerPoint. 9/10

Note: Obsidian and Notion are amazing Word alternatives.

Media production

OBS Studio - Open-source software for video recording and live streaming. I use it for my streams. 10/10
DaVinci Resolve - Video editing software. 8/10

Adobe Photoshop - Photoshop, just photoshop. I love every single function and use it a lot. Also I really like its AI 9/10
Adobe After Effects - Video editing software. 8/10
Adobe Premiere Pro - Video editing software. 8/10
Adobe Lightroom - Photo editing and organizing software. I use it for its image refining. And management of the photos. 8/10
GIMP - Free and open-source image editing software. I prefer Photoshop. 7/10
